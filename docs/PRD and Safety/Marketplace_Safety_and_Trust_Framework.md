# Rent-a-Friend Marketplace Safety & Trust Framework

## Product Governance, Safety Controls & Marketplace Policies (Version 1.0)

This document defines the safety architecture, operational controls, and
marketplace policies for a platonic, professional local companion
platform.

### 1. Structural System Architecture & Technical Controls

#### Identity Verification

**Guides (Mandatory):** - Government-issued ID verification -
Selfie/liveness verification - Phone and email verification - Payment
verification - Emergency contact registration - Background screening
where legally available - Periodic re-verification

**Seekers (Primary Booker - Mandatory):** - Government-issued ID
verification - Selfie/liveness verification - Phone verification - Email
verification - Payment verification

#### Group Registration

Every booking stores: - Booking ID - Primary Seeker - Total group size -
Full name of every attendee - Phone number of every attendee - Optional
nationality - 18+ confirmation

Only registered attendees are covered.

#### Group Variance Policy

If additional attendees appear, the Guide may either continue or reject
the booking. If rejected: - Booking ends immediately. - No refund to the
Seeker. - Guide receives payment according to platform policy.

#### Meeting Verification

A unique 4-digit PIN is generated for each booking.

Flow: Guide Arrives → Seeker Shares PIN → Guide Enters PIN → Booking
Starts → GPS Starts → Timer Starts

Without the PIN: - Booking cannot begin. - Timer does not start. -
Payment remains in escrow.

#### Automatic GPS Tracking

-   Guide GPS automatically activates when the booking begins.
-   If location services are disabled, the system issues an immediate
    warning.
-   Continued failure pauses the booking and records a violation.

#### Optional Seeker Location Sharing

-   Optional live location during the booking.
-   Automatically expires after booking.

#### Communication Sandboxing

Automatically blocks: - Phone numbers - Email addresses - Social media
usernames - QR codes - External payment links - Crypto wallets -
Off-platform booking attempts

#### Escrow Payments

Payment Flow: Seeker → Platform Escrow → Booking Completion → Guide
Payment

No cash or off-platform payments permitted.

#### Emergency Tools

-   Emergency button
-   Notify platform safety team
-   Share live location
-   Emergency contact notification
-   Incident reporting

#### Safe Meeting Locations

Recommended: - Museums - Cafés - Shopping malls - Parks - Tourist
attractions - Public transport hubs

#### Audit Logs

Immutable logs include: - Booking - Chat - Payment - GPS - PIN -
Reviews - Disputes

### 2. Core Policies for Guides

#### Mandatory

-   Professional conduct
-   Public locations only
-   Never enter hotel rooms or private residences (except verified
    emergencies)
-   Strictly platonic service
-   Group variance policy
-   No off-platform payments
-   Professional appearance
-   No illegal activities
-   No weapons where prohibited by platform policy

#### Recommended

-   No alcohol or drugs during bookings
-   Route planning
-   15-minute grace period
-   Maintain personal boundaries

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 3. Core Policies for Seekers

#### Mandatory

-   Respect Guides
-   Register every attendee accurately
-   Cover agreed activity-related costs (tickets, transport, meals where
    applicable)
-   Do not require personal vehicle transport
-   Respect service boundaries
-   No off-platform booking or payment requests

#### Recommended

-   Temporary refundable security authorization
-   Register emergency contact
-   Share itinerary
-   Arrive 10--15 minutes early

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 4. System Integrity & Corporate Fairness

#### Mandatory

Cancellation Matrix:

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Cancellation Time       Seeker Refund           Guide Compensation
  ----------------------- ----------------------- -----------------------
  \>48 Hours              Full                    None

  24--48 Hours            Partial                 Partial

  \<24 Hours              No Refund (except       Full/Substantial
                          approved emergencies)   

  Guide Cancels           Full Refund             Penalty
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

Additional policies: - No-show handling - Two-way blind reviews -
Evidence-based dispute resolution - Progressive enforcement - Appeals
process

#### Recommended

-   Encrypted opt-in safety audio recording
-   Automated trust score
-   Low-rating quality review
-   Community safety relief fund
-   Periodic reverification

## Platform Commitment

The platform commits to: - Verified identities - Secure escrow
payments - Public-space meetings - Transparent investigations - Fair
protection for both Seekers and Guides - Consistent enforcement - A
strictly platonic marketplace for cultural exploration and local
companionship.
