1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This document establishes the updated governance, policy, and product guardrails
for our double-sided marketplace. It incorporates strict behavioral rules, data 
collection standards, automated physical location tracking mechanisms, and a clear 
operational response framework to handle group size variance and safety flags.

The platform's baseline requirement remains unchanged: This is a platonic, 
companionship-only service designed to accompany stays in a specific city, 
and nothing more.

================================================================================
2. STRUCTURAL SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE & TECHNICAL CONTROLS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A. Identity Verification (KYC) & Group Data Collection
   - Seeker KYC Mandate: The primary booking user ("Booker") must pass full Know Your 
     Customer (KYC) protocols, including government ID/passport uploading and a biometric 
     liveness selfie scan prior to booking validation.
   - Group Manifest Requirements: The system requires the Booker to input the exact 
     number of attendees along with the full legal names and active phone numbers of 
     every individual participating in the tour. Anonymous or unregistered guests 
     are systemically prohibited.

B. Automated Live Location Tracking Framework
   - Mandatory Guide Geofencing: Background GPS tracking is strictly mandatory for 
     all Guides during active booking hours. 
   - Clock-Activated Automation: The moment the scheduled meeting time arrives, the 
     platform application automatically forces the Guide's device GPS tracking into the 
     "On" status. This telemetry is recorded continuously to the server logs.
   - Proactive Warning System: If the booking start time triggers and the platform 
     cannot ping the Guide's live location (due to permission withdrawal or device 
     shutdown), the system automatically fires an urgent, escalating in-app alert 
     and SMS warning. If unresolved within 10 minutes, the booking is flagged for 
     immediate support intervention.
   - Optional Seeker Tracking: To enhance confidence and facilitate seamless physical 
     rendezvous in crowded city hubs, Seekers can toggle on temporary, mutual 
     location sharing at the meeting start time.

C. Communication Sandboxing
   - In-app text filters actively block external communication handles (e.g., sharing 
     WhatsApp, Telegram, or personal numbers) prior to a locked booking. Financial escrow 
     holds funds securely until 24 hours post-trip.

3. CORE POLICIES FOR THE LOCALS (GUIDES / FRIENDS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A. COMPULSORY POLICIES (Strictly Enforced)

   1. Group Variance & Attendance Discrepancy Policy
      - Definition: The Guide is explicitly contracted to accompany only the specific individuals 
        registered on the booking manifest. 
      - Guide's Right to Terminate: If the number of people who arrive at the meeting point 
        exceeds the manifest, or if the individuals present vary entirely from the registered 
        names, the Guide possesses the absolute right to terminate the request immediately.
      - Financial Protection: In the event of a group variance termination, the booking charge 
        is completely non-refundable to the Seeker, and the Guide receives their full payout.
      - Exception Clause: If the Guide assesses the situation, feels completely safe, and 
        voluntarily consents to accommodate the extra/changed people, they have the option to 
        manually override and continue the tour.

   2. Boundary, Location, & Conduct Rules
      - Zero-tolerance ban on entering a Seeker’s private accommodation (hotel rooms, 
        Airbnbs, residential properties). Tours must be conducted in public or commercial spaces.
      - Complete prohibition on romantic or sexual advances. All activity must be platonic.
      - Absolute ban on accepting off-platform financial transactions (cash, personal peer-to-peer apps).

B. NON-COMPULSORY BUT EFFECTIVE POLICIES (Best Practices)

   1. Complete Alcohol & Substance Abstinence Policy
      - Guides are strongly advised to reject any alcoholic beverages offered by the Seeker 
        and remain completely sober throughout the itinerary. Absolute zero-tolerance for 
        illegal or controlled substances.

   2. Local Emergency Route Pre-Vetting
      - Guides are encouraged to plan routes that pass through major, open commercial 
        corridors rather than isolated alleys, prioritizing safety in unfamiliar city sectors.

================================================================================
4. CORE POLICIES FOR THE SEEKERS (TRAVELERS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A. COMPULSORY POLICIES (Strictly Enforced)

   1. Accurate Guest Manifest & Anti-Baiting Mandate
      - Seekers are legally required to provide accurate, truthful data regarding group count 
        and attendee names. Bringing unlisted persons to a booking constitutes a breach of 
        the terms of service.

   2. Behavioral Boundaries & Incidental Cost Ownership
      - Zero-harassment mandate. Explicit language, stalking, or inappropriate contact yields 
        an immediate ban with no refund.
      - The Seeker covers 100% of the incidental costs generated during the itinerary (e.g., 
        the Guide’s entrance tickets, public transit passes, meals, and drinks during the session).
      - Seekers cannot mandate or require the Guide to ride in or operate an unvetted private vehicle.

B. NON-COMPULSORY BUT EFFECTIVE POLICIES (Best Practices)

   1. Pre-Authorized Incidentals and Late-Extension Deposit
      - Implement a temporary $50 credit authorization hold on the Seeker's card to cover 
        authorized extra hours or unexpected venue additions without manual re-billing.

5. SYSTEM INTEGRITY & ADDITIONAL SUGGESTIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A. New Suggestion: Real-Time Verification Pins (Check-In Guard)
   - At the physical meeting location, the app generates a unique 4-digit PIN on the Seeker's 
     screen. The Guide must input this PIN into their own app to formally initiate the tour 
     and unlock the live tracking map. This proves both parties are physically present together.

B. New Suggestion: Discreet Safety Audio Recording
   - Introduce an opt-in safety feature inside the app allowing either user to trigger 
     encrypted, short-burst audio recordings if they feel an interaction is turning hostile 
     or violating terms. This audio is sent directly to platform trust agents for review.

C. Two-Way Blind Review System & Balanced Cancellations
   - Reviews are hidden until both sides fill them out to stop retaliatory down-voting. 
   - Cancellations under 24 hours protect the local's opportunity cost by withholding refunds.
